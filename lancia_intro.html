Lancia has been building cars since 1906.

It has won more world rally championship titles than any other car manufacturer, had the first standard production V6, the first load bearing bodyshell and the first electrical system in a car.

This web site aims to provide information on all the cars developed and built by Lancia plus its prototypes. Information on the company, its motorsport activities and history are joined by a host of other features which should be of interest to the owner, mechanic, enthusiast or general reader.

There are 32 model specific pages which cover all production models since 1922, up to and including current production.
For each model there is an overview, technical details, illustrations, links, your comments and more.

Including the new, 2008, Delta.
